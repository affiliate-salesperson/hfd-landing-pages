# Health Flow Daily™ — Style Guide

Aesthetic:
- Minimalist
- Clean
- Quiet
- Elegant
- Editorial wellness style

Colors:
- White (#FFFFFF)
- Soft Gray (#F7F7F7)
- Warm Beige (#F8F5F0)
- Charcoal Text (#2B2B2B)

Typography:
- Inter, Lato, or Nunito Sans

Spacing:
- Large vertical padding
- Wide breathing room
- Clear visual hierarchy

Imagery:
- Abstract, soft, geometric
- No photos of people

Do not deviate from this.
